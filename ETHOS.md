# ETHOS — Why We Are Building the First Indie Glass Drive

We are not waiting for SPhotonix or Microsoft to sell us $30k toys.

We are indie hackers. We use COTS gear. We ship ugly v0.1 that works.

This project exists to prove that eternal data storage — data that survives billions of years — does not have to live behind patents, NDAs, or venture-capital gates.

One Raspberry Pi + one xTool F2 Ultra UV + some fused silica = a glass drive anyone can own.

We document everything publicly from day one.
We celebrate janky prototypes.
We merge PRs from teenagers in their garage.

If you ship even 1 GB of data that survives a house fire, you are legend.

This is batshit ambitious.
This is exactly how everything that actually changes the world gets built.
